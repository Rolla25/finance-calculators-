Important Disclaimer

This calculator is intended for educational and illustrative purposes only and assumes a constant rate of return throughout the withdrawal period. Actual investment returns are unlikely to be consistent and may vary significantly over time.

For market-linked investments such as mutual funds, stocks, ETFs, and other securities, portfolio values can fluctuate due to market conditions. During periods of weak or negative returns, withdrawals may reduce the principal investment faster than projected, causing the corpus to deplete sooner than estimated.

The calculations provided are estimates only and should not be interpreted as a guarantee of future performance, returns, or corpus longevity. Actual outcomes may differ due to market volatility, sequence of returns risk, taxes, expenses, inflation, and changes in withdrawal patterns.

Users should use the results as indicative projections and not as financial advice or a prediction of future investment performance.
