Frequently Asked Questions

Are the calculations accurate?
Yes. Every calculator is built using standard financial formulas that are widely used by investors, financial planners, banks, and spreadsheet based financial models. Wherever applicable, the formulas are based on commonly accepted methodologies used in finance and investing.
Are the results guaranteed?
No. Calculator outputs are estimates based on the assumptions and inputs you provide. Actual investment returns, inflation rates, interest rates, taxes, and market conditions may differ from the assumptions used in the calculations.
Do I need to create an account to use the calculators?
No. All calculators on Corpusly are available without registration or sign up. You can use them freely without creating an account.
Do you store my financial data?
No. Calculations run directly in your browser. We do not require personal financial information, and your calculator inputs are not stored on our servers.
Which assumptions should I use for returns and inflation?
Conservative assumptions generally provide more realistic projections. If you are unsure, consider using long term historical averages and testing multiple scenarios to understand how different assumptions affect the results.
Are these calculators financial advice?
No. Corpusly calculators are educational and informational tools designed to help with financial planning and analysis. They should not be considered investment, tax, insurance, or financial advice. Important financial decisions should be made after considering your personal circumstances and, where appropriate, consulting a qualified professional.
