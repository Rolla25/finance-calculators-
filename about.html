About Corpusly

Smarter Financial Decisions Start with Better Calculations

Corpusly is a free financial calculator platform designed to help individuals make informed decisions about investing, retirement planning, insurance, and personal finance.

Financial decisions often involve complex calculations, assumptions, and long term projections. Our goal is to simplify those calculations and make them accessible to everyone through easy to use, transparent, and reliable tools.

Whether you're estimating SIP returns, planning retirement, evaluating insurance needs, calculating investment performance, or projecting long term wealth creation, Corpusly provides practical calculators that help transform financial concepts into actionable insights.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple:

To make financial planning more accessible by providing free, accurate, and easy to use financial calculators.

We believe that better financial decisions begin with a clear understanding of the numbers behind them.

Our Approach

Every calculator on Corpusly is built using widely accepted financial formulas and methodologies commonly used by investors, financial planners, financial institutions, and spreadsheet software.

We aim to provide:

  • Transparent calculations
  • Clear assumptions
  • Practical outputs
  • Easy to understand results

Transparency and Trust

We believe users should understand how results are generated.

For this reason, we focus on transparency in our assumptions, methodologies, and calculations whenever possible.

While we strive for accuracy, all calculator outputs should be viewed as estimates based on user provided inputs and assumptions.
