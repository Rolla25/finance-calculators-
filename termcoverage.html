How This Amount Was Calculated

This estimate is designed to help ensure your family can maintain their lifestyle if you are no longer around. The recommended cover takes into account:

  • Future household expenses until your chosen life expectancy, growing annually with inflation
  • Any remaining loan EMI obligations, funded year by year until each loan ends
  • Future financial goals adjusted individually for inflation
  • Your existing investment corpus, which reduces the cover required

The recommended cover represents the estimated lump sum required today so your family can meet these obligations, assuming the proceeds are invested at the assumed post-death return rate.

⚠️ This calculator provides an estimate for educational purposes and should not be considered financial advice. Actual insurance needs may vary based on income growth, future savings, changing family circumstances, inflation, investment returns, and other factors. Please consult a qualified financial advisor before purchasing insurance.
